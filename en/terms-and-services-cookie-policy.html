Functions of the cookies used on our site

Cookies are short text files that are stored in the end user's device and are used by the user while surfing the Internet.
They help to identify the given device and, based on individual information, enable the appropriate display and customization of websites.
‍
A cookie usually contains the name of the website from which the cookie originates, information about its lifetime and a unique number.
‍
Absolutely necessary cookies are required for the operation of the website and its various functions.

If these are disabled, the website does not work properly, for example, the usual services of the website become unavailable.
‍
These cookies do not retain data collected during browsing.
